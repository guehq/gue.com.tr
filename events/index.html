KarstDive: Western Taurus Project project

We're thrilled to announce the KarstDive: Western Taurus Cave Project, an ambitious exploration and documentation initiative in the breathtaking cave systems of the Western Taurus Mountains, Türkiye. Join us as we delve into the unknown, contributing valuable data to science, conservation, and diving exploration. Stay tuned for updates and incredible visuals from the heart of Türkiye's karst wonderland.

Certification requirement: T3, CCR-C and above

Finike, Antalya / Türkiye

Gallipoli Wrecks Week diving trip

Dive into history as we visit five iconic wrecks from the Gallipoli Campaign, immerse ourselves in the stories behind these sites, and experience the beauty of the Çanakkale region. Between dives, we'll visit local museums to further connect with the area's heritage, and enjoy the region's renowned cuisine. Stay tuned for more details, including the dive itinerary and booking information!

Certification requirement: T1 and above

Kocatepe, Çanakkale / Türkiye
