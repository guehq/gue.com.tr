Our Club on Strava

Challenge Rules

Dates: August 1–31, 2025 (inclusive)

1. Participation Requirements

  • Participants must use the Strava platform and follow the GUE Club account. The participant list will be generated based on this club.
  • Participants must also connect with Nevcan Uludaş on Strava for manual activity verification.

2. Timeframe and Activity Validity

  • The challenge starts at 00:00 on August 1 and ends at 23:59 on August 31 (local time).
  • The start time of each activity will be considered for validity.
  • Backdated entries will not be accepted. For example, an activity performed on August 2 but uploaded on August 3 will not count.

3. Daily Activity Requirements

  • Participants must complete at least one valid activity per day throughout all 31 days.
  • The minimum duration for a valid activity is 30 minutes.
  • Multiple activities in a single day are allowed if each meets the minimum duration.

4. Activity Types and Evaluation Criteria

  • All types of physical activities are accepted (e.g., walking, running, cycling, swimming, yoga, fitness).
  • Evaluation is based on total activity duration (in minutes).
  • Suspicious activities may be invalidated by the GUE Marketing Team. This does not disqualify the participant from the overall challenge—only the specific activity will be excluded.

5. Monitoring and Fair Play

  • Manual entries and unusual data will be reviewed by the GUE Marketing Team.
  • Non-intentional daily movements (e.g., walking to the store) will not count as valid activities.

Optional Categories (for extra engagement)

These optional recognitions may be highlighted at the end of the challenge:

  • Longest Total Distance
  • Highest Elevation Gain
  • Most Frequent Athlete (number of valid activities)
  • Most Creative Activity Post (with photo)
  • MET-based scoring
  • Team competitions

Additional formats such as MET-based scoring and team competitions may be introduced later.
